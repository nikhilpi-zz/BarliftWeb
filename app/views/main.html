Drink Spontaneously

BarLift sends you awesome deals from bars and restaurants around you.
See who’s interested in going out, nudge friends and find out where the crowds are headed.

BarLift is an app that sends you exclusive drink deals and freebies from bars & restaurants located around you. We’re available for iOS and Android and currently live at Northwestern.

Deals are sent in real time, giving you spontaneous options throughout the night. In-app social features enable you to stay connected with the people around you, allowing you to see who’s interested in going out and where the crowds are headed.

Questions or comments/feedback? Shoot us a message.
